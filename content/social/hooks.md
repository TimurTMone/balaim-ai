# Opening Hooks That Convert

Use these as first lines. They stop the scroll.

## Curiosity Hooks
- "Things no one tells you about [X]..."
- "I had 12 pregnancy apps. Here's what I learned..."
- "The 2am google search every new parent makes..."
- "Why your pediatrician never tells you this..."

## Contrarian Hooks
- "Everyone says X. Here's why they're wrong."
- "Stop doing [common thing]. Do this instead."
- "The one thing I wish I'd ignored during pregnancy..."
- "Old wives' tales that are actually dangerous..."

## Specific/Number Hooks
- "7 things I Googled at 3am that turned out to be normal"
- "The 4 signs of labor that matter"
- "Week 24 in 30 seconds"
- "The $0 parenting hack that actually works"

## Emotional Hooks
- "If you're crying at your desk wondering if this is normal — read this"
- "The text I sent my partner at 2am that changed everything"
- "I don't post this to scare you. I post it because someone needs to hear it."

## Question Hooks
- "Is your baby doing this? Here's what it means..."
- "Should you be worried about [symptom]? Depends..."
- "Why is no one telling new parents [X]?"

## Founder Hooks
- "Building in public: month 1 of [project]"
- "The moment I knew I had to build this..."
- "5 things I've learned shipping a startup with a newborn..."
- "Why I'm quitting my job to build [project]..."

## Bold Statement Hooks
- "Most pregnancy apps are lying to you."
- "Your doctor doesn't have time to explain this."
- "The parenting industry is built on anxiety. It shouldn't be."

## Data Hooks
- "95% of babies don't arrive on their due date. Here's when they DO arrive..."
- "Your baby's brain forms 1 million neural connections per second. At week 4."
- "10 kicks in 2 hours = safe. Here's why this number matters..."
