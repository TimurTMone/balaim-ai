# Balam.AI Content

Marketing and social content drawn directly from the app's pregnancy and baby development data. All facts are AAP/WHO/CDC aligned — the same sources used in the app.

## Structure

```
content/
├── social/              # Ready-to-post social content
│   ├── instagram-posts.md       # 30 Instagram/TikTok caption templates
│   ├── twitter-threads.md       # 5 long-form threads
│   ├── content-calendar.md      # 4-week launch calendar
│   └── hooks.md                 # Opening lines that convert
└── README.md
```

## Philosophy

- **Value first.** Every post teaches something useful. No fluff.
- **Evidence-based.** Every fact comes from the app's curated content, which cites AAP, WHO, CDC.
- **Warm, not clinical.** Speak like a knowledgeable friend.
- **Parent-first.** Moms AND dads. Inclusive language.
- **Disclaimers when relevant.** Never diagnose. Always point to a doctor for concerns.

## Posting Cadence (Recommended)

| Platform | Cadence | Content Type |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| Instagram | 3-4/week | Carousels + reels from weekly tips |
| TikTok | 3-5/week | Short-form: "Did you know..." + pregnancy myths |
| Twitter/X | Daily | Threads + quick tips |
| LinkedIn | 1-2/week | Founder story + building in public |

## Connecting to the App

All content lives in the app. When possible, include CTA:
> 💜 Full week-by-week in Balam.AI — link in bio
